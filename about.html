About

Hello there.

My name is Henrique Périgo; I am currently the lead interactive developer at Sosolimited, a Design and Architecture studio based in Boston - MA that works creating sensory spaces driven by design and technology.

I'm a technical polyglot with a passion in Motion and Computational Design; I've lead projects that range from data-driven sculptures using C++. Developed graphics and interation systems for touch screens applications with OpenGL, animated TV spots, and created 3d animations for large scale projection mapping.
